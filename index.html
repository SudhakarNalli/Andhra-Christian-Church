Our Ministries

Get involved. We have groups and spaces designed for all ages and walks of life.

Grace Kids (Ages 0-11)

Creating a safe, fun, and engaging environment where kids can learn about Jesus on their level.

Our background-checked leaders provide interactive lessons, energetic worship, and age-appropriate games every Sunday morning during services. We partner with parents to help kids grow their faith in a loving environment.

Register Your Child

Sundays at 9:00 & 11:00 AM

Check-in opens 15 minutes before service starts.

Grace Youth (Grades 6-12)

Empowering teenagers to discover their identity in Christ and build lifelong, faith-filled friendships.

We meet weekly for games, high-energy worship, and deep small group discussions about real-life issues. It's a place where every teenager is welcome, accepted, and encouraged to lead.

Join Youth Groups

Wednesdays at 7:00 PM

In the Youth Auditorium. Includes cafe and hangouts.

Young Adults (Ages 18-30)

Navigating college, careers, and life decisions in a community committed to following Christ.

Our YA Ministry hosts monthly gatherings, small groups throughout the city, and social events. We are passionate about searching for truth, serving together, and having deep discussions about faith in the modern world.

Get Connected

Every Tuesday at 7:30 PM

At local coffee shops and home groups. Join anytime.

Outreach & Missions

Serving our local community and sharing the hope of Christ globally.

We partner with local shelters, food pantries, and global mission organizations to support people in need. From weekly food distributions to seasonal service projects, there is a place for you to make a tangible difference.
