Who We Are

Our Fellowship

Andhra Christian Church in Mumbai is a vibrant fellowship of believers sharing fellowship, faith, and prayer in Andheri East.

Established as a spiritual sanctuary for Telugu-speaking and diverse Christian communities in Mumbai, we gather to worship in truth, support one another in love, and nurture the spiritual growth of all generations.

Whether you are visiting, seeking a spiritual home, or looking for prayer support, our doors and hearts are open to you.
